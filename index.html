Welcome to Palermo Pizzeria

Nestled in the heart of Montreal, "Palermo Pizzeria" is a beacon of tradition and family heritage. Established in 1947 by Guiseppe Fontana, a World War II veteran, the pizzeria has been a cornerstone of authentic Italian flavors for over seven decades.

After returning from the war, Guiseppe's determination led him to create a haven that would encapsulate the essence of his homeland. In a small space with an old wood-fired oven, Palermo Pizzeria was born. The aroma of freshly baked pizzas mingled with the laughter of patrons, creating a welcoming ambiance that felt like home.

Over the years, Palermo Pizzeria embraced change while preserving its core values. Guiseppe's children joined the venture, infusing their own creativity while honoring their father's recipes. The pizzeria's legacy expanded, drawing in generations of loyal patrons who were captivated by the authentic Italian flavors.

Today, Palermo Pizzeria remains a tribute to family, tradition, and unwavering passion. Each pizza is a masterpiece, a testament to Guiseppe's dedication to preserving the flavors of his heritage. The pizzeria's walls are adorned with faded photographs, telling stories of its rich history.
